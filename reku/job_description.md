# Senior Growth Product Manager - Reku

## About Us

We are building a leading consumer social application with global reach and rapid adoption among Gen Alpha users. The product is already ranked among the top consumer apps worldwide and continues to scale quickly. This is an opportunity to join an early but proven team and play a critical role in shaping the next phase of growth.

We are seeking a Senior Growth Product Manager to lead product-led growth initiatives and drive activation, retention, and scale across the platform.

## Responsibilities

- Own and lead the product-led growth strategy and roadmap
- Design and optimize viral loops, onboarding flows, and retention mechanisms
- Define and analyze growth funnels, cohorts, and key performance metrics
- Run structured experiments to improve activation, engagement, and DAU/MAU
- Partner closely with engineering, design, and marketing to execute growth initiatives
- Translate user behavior insights into scalable product improvements

## Requirements

- 5 to 8+ years of product management experience, with a significant focus on growth or consumer-facing products
- Extensive experience in consumer product management with a growth orientation
- Strong data fluency, including hands-on use of tools such as Mixpanel or Amplitude
- Proven track record building and scaling product-led growth loops
- Experience working on high-scale consumer platforms (e.g., social, gaming, or messaging products)
- Demonstrated success operating in both early-stage (0 to 1) and scaling (10 to 100) product environments
- Strong experimentation mindset with the ability to move quickly and iterate
- Founder-level ownership mentality and comfort operating in ambiguous environments

## Preferred Qualifications

- Background at leading consumer technology companies
- Experience with viral mechanics, network effects, and social product dynamics
- Prior startup or founder experience

## What This Role Offers

- High ownership and direct impact on company-wide growth outcomes
- Opportunity to define growth strategy at a global consumer scale
- Collaboration with a seasoned team from top consumer technology companies
- Fast-paced environment with strong product velocity and clear user demand
