# Senior Manager, Data Strategy & Analytics - Happy Money

## About Happy Money

Happy Money is a consumer finance company that empowers people to achieve their goals through simple, transparent loans funded by trusted financial institutions. Through its proprietary lending platform, Hive, Happy Money delivers a fully digital experience that simplifies borrowing for consumers and helps credit unions, banks and asset managers diversify balance sheets and scale with confidence.

Happy Money's end-to-end loan origination and participation programs provide partners with turnkey access to high-performing assets, underpinned by disciplined credit expertise and robust risk management. Together with its lending partners, Happy Money has originated more than $6.5 billion in loans to help nearly 350,000 people take greater control of their financial futures.

## About The Role

Happy Money is looking for a Senior Manager, Data Strategy & Analytics to join our Risk/Credit team. In this role, you'll serve as a strategic analytics partner across the organization, translating complex data into actionable insights that drive decision-making across Credit, Operations, Marketing, Product, and Finance. You'll work with large datasets in a modern cloud-based environment, building dashboards, developing analytical models, and uncovering trends that help us deliver on our mission of helping people achieve financial wellness through responsible personal lending.

## Responsibilities

- Write efficient, well-documented SQL queries in Snowflake to extract, transform, and analyze large datasets
- Develop and automate analytical workflows using Python and/or R, with code managed through GitHub
- Design, build, and maintain dashboards and reports in Tableau that provide visibility into key business metrics across Credit, Operations, Marketing, and Finance
- Partner with Data Engineering to build and refine data workflows in Databricks that support reporting and analytics needs
- Conduct ad hoc analyses to answer strategic business questions, presenting findings with clear recommendations to leadership
- Monitor data quality and proactively identify anomalies or issues in key metrics
- Develop and document analytical methodologies, continuously evaluating opportunities to optimize processes and introduce improved approaches

## About You

- 7+ years of experience in business intelligence, data analytics, or a related field, preferably within financial services or FinTech
- Strong SQL skills with experience querying cloud data warehouses such as Snowflake
- Proficiency in Tableau or similar visualization tools
- Working knowledge of Python and/or R for data manipulation, analysis, and automation
- Familiarity with Databricks, Spark, or similar distributed computing platforms
- Experience using Git/GitHub for version control and collaborative development
- Excellent communication skills with the ability to distill complex analyses into clear narratives

## Bonus Points For

- Experience in consumer lending, credit risk modeling, or the personal loan industry
- Passion for leveraging AI tools and generative AI to enhance analytics workflows
- Familiarity with dbt or modern analytics engineering practices
- Exposure to machine learning concepts or predictive modeling techniques
- Knowledge of regulatory requirements in financial services (e.g., fair lending, FCRA)
- Experience presenting insights and recommendations to executive leadership
