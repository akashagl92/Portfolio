# Senior Manager, Marketing Analytics - Kraken (Breakout)

## Building the Future of Crypto

Our Krakenites are a world-class team with crypto conviction, united by our desire to discover and unlock the potential of crypto and blockchain technology.

## The Team: Breakout

Breakout is a crypto-native prop trading platform backed by Kraken. Founded in 2023 by industry veterans, we combine deep liquidity, cutting-edge technology, and world-class risk management to create an unparalleled trading experience. Recently acquired by Kraken, this acquisition gains the benefit of Kraken’s global infrastructure and trusted brand.

## The Opportunity

As Senior Manager, Marketing Analytics, you’ll be the architect of Breakout Prop’s marketing data ecosystem. You’ll build the tracking infrastructure, reporting frameworks, and analytical foundation that turn marketing data into insight — and insight into action. This is a unique opportunity to design a world-class analytics function from the ground up.

## Responsibilities

- **Build the foundation**: Design and implement a reliable, scalable marketing data infrastructure, ensuring accurate tagging, attribution, and data flow across channels.
- **Implement tracking systems**: Own and optimize tools like Google Tag Manager, Segment, or Tealium to ensure end-to-end visibility of the user journey.
- **Develop dashboards & reporting**: Create intuitive, automated dashboards and visualizations in platforms like Tableau, Looker, or Periscope.
- **Analyze and interpret performance**: Lead analyses that uncover drivers of acquisition, activation, retention, and LTV.
- **Investigate data abnormalities**: Proactively investigate data issues so that by the time leadership see it, there is an explanation.
- **Model and forecast**: Build and refine attribution models, cohort conversion studies, CLTV frameworks, and response models.
- **Partner cross-functionally**: Collaborate with Growth Marketing, Product, and Finance to ensure a single source of truth.
- **Champion data culture**: Establish best practices for data governance, measurement frameworks, and experimentation standards.

## Skills You Should HODL

- 5+ years of experience in marketing analytics, ideally in a growth-stage or digital-first company.
- Exceptional quantitative and analytical skills with deep fluency in interpreting marketing and growth data.
- Expert knowledge of digital marketing analytics — attribution models, CLTV modeling, response modeling, and cohort analysis.
- Intermediate SQL skills and proficiency in at least one major BI platform (Tableau, Looker, or Periscope).
- Advanced experience with Google Analytics, Adobe Analytics, or Heap, and tag management platforms (Google Tag Manager, Segment, or Tealium).
- Strong understanding of paid media, web analytics, and user journey tracking.
- Proven ability to translate complex data into clear, actionable insights for both technical and non-technical audiences.
- A systems thinker who thrives in startup environments.
- Curiosity and enthusiasm for the crypto ecosystem.
